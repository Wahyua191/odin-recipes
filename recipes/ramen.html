Ramen Recipe

Description

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup dish that consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat- or fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and uses toppings such as sliced pork, nori (dried seaweed), menma (bamboo shoots), and green onions.

Ingredients

  • 200g fresh ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken or pork broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 100g sliced pork belly
  • 1 sheet nori (dried seaweed)
  • 50g menma (bamboo shoots)
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. In a pot, bring the broth to a simmer and add soy sauce and miso paste.
  2. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions, then drain.
  3. In serving bowls, place the cooked noodles and pour the hot broth over them.
  4. Top with sliced pork, boiled eggs, nori, menma, green onions, and sesame oil.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade ramen!
